Electricity and Magnetism | MGC Tech Tips

Electric Circuit Theory and Electromagnetic theory are two fundamental theories at which all branches of Electronics Engineering are built. Branches like power, electrical machines, electronics, communications, instrumentation are all base in electric circuit theory. Hence, this lecture is one of the most important part as an Electronics Engineering Student or as a full pledge Engineer. This lecture course is an excellent starting point as a beginner in this field.
